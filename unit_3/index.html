Consultants in Groundwater and Wetland Science

Rigare was established in September 2008 to provide high-quality, independent, expert technical consultant and research services in the areas of groundwater and wetland hydrology. We also offer a small range of specialist wetland hydrological equipment for purchase or hire, and specialist groundwater tracer test design and execution, making use of our in-house spectrofluorimeter.

The company is based in Abergavenny, Wales, and has successfully completed projects worldwide.
